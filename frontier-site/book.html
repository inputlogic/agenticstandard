Honest Fit

Who this is for, and who it isn't.

A consulting firm that doesn't disqualify is one that takes work it shouldn't. So before you book, here's what fits and what doesn't.

Strong fit

If you're here for this, we should talk.

  • You have an executive sponsor who can approve a $48k commitment
  • You've tried something with AI that didn't stick; you understand the gap is real
  • Your pain is framed as capacity ("we can’t take on X without more people") more than as productivity
  • Your decision window is 30–90 days, not 6+ months
  • You operate in Slack or Teams as your primary collaboration layer
  • Your stack is modern enough that agents can read and write to your tools
Not our fit

If you're here for these, the referral goes elsewhere.

  • You want a strategy deck without deployment
  • "Tell us what AI to use" framing; this means you need readiness work first
  • A failed implementation with vendor blame in flight (we do not litigate other firms' work)
  • Decision involves 6+ stakeholders not yet in the room
  • Sub-$40k budget with no flexibility
  • Heavily regulated environment (HIPAA, PCI primary) requiring compliance work beyond our practice

If you're somewhere in the middle, please book a call. The conversation will resolve it faster than reading more pages of this site.
