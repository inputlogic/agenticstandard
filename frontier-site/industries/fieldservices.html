Agent 01

The Dispatch Triage

Sorts urgent from routine, matches techs to jobs by skill and route, and drafts the customer confirmations. Dispatch approves and sends.

Worker multiplier8×Triages every request, matches every tech, drafts each confirm.

How it works

The loop: Runs on every request. Reads the booking inbox and the FSM board. Sorts urgency, suggests the tech, drafts confirmations. Dispatch approves each one.

What it needs:

  • Read: the booking inbox
  • Draft: confirmations, approval-gated
  • Owner: your dispatch manager
  • Spend cap enforced in the request path
  • Audit log on from day one
Agent 02

The Quote Runner

Chases every aging quote at the right cadence, logs the replies, and drops a close-rate digest every Friday.

Worker multiplier7×Chases every aging quote at cadence, none goes cold.

How it works

The loop: Runs daily. Reads open quotes from the FSM system. Sends follow-ups at the right cadence. Logs replies, posts Friday's close-rate digest. Humans set the price.

What it needs:

  • Read: the open-quote report
  • Send: follow-ups from a scoped mailbox
  • Owner: your service manager
  • Per-run budget cap, enforced
  • Every send logged
Agent 03

The Warranty Clerk

Assembles claims paperwork with serials and photos attached, submits on approval, and tracks every claim to payout.

Worker multiplier6×Assembles every warranty claim, tracks each to payout.

How it works

The loop: Runs on every eligible job. Reads work orders, serials, and photos. Assembles the claim packet. Queues it for approval, tracks payout. Humans approve.

What it needs:

  • Read: closed work orders and photos
  • Post: the warranty tracking sheet
  • Owner: your office manager
  • Budget cap live in the request path
  • Audit trail from day one

The multiplier shows up on the road: the same office keeps more trucks rolling full days, and no quote ever goes cold.
