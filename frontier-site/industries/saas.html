Agent 01

The Signal Spotter

Watches trial and usage data for product-qualified moments, then posts the digest to your AEs in Slack, account by account.

Worker multiplier8×Reads usage on every account, hourly, misses no spike.

How it works

The loop: Runs hourly. Reads product usage events. Flags product-qualified moments by account. Posts the digest to the sales channel. AEs pick up the phone.

What it needs:

  • Read: the product analytics view
  • Post: #pql-signals in Slack
  • Owner: your RevOps lead
  • Spend cap enforced per run
  • Every query logged
Agent 02

The Renewal Briefer

Builds a churn-risk brief before every renewal call. Usage trend, open tickets, stakeholder changes, and the one question worth asking.

Worker multiplier5×Briefs every renewal, not just the big accounts.

How it works

The loop: Runs a week before each renewal. Reads usage, tickets, and CRM notes. Drafts the one-page brief. Posts it to the account channel. The CSM leads the call.

What it needs:

  • Read: the CRM renewal view
  • Post: per-account Slack channels
  • Owner: your head of CS
  • Budget cap live in the request path
  • Audit log from day one
Agent 03

The Friction Mapper

Clusters the week's support tickets into themes and hands product a ranked friction report every Friday.

Worker multiplier4×Reads every ticket weekly, not a Friday sample.

How it works

The loop: Runs Fridays. Reads the week's support tickets. Clusters them into themes, ranks by volume. Posts the report to product. Humans set the roadmap.

What it needs:

  • Read: the support ticket export
  • Post: #product-friction in Slack
  • Owner: your product ops manager
  • Per-run spend cap, enforced
  • Every run logged
